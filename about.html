Our Mission 
To raise community awareness of and promote public support for issues related to environmental protection, energy, nature conservation and sustainable development. 
Our Vision 
A world which enjoys an environment that is both healthy and pleasant, in which the community places a premium on sustaining such an environment for both themselves and future generations, and pursues sustainable development.
Community 
By changing the way you use products and resources you can prevent pollution and save money. Preventing pollution provides cleaner air and water, less waste in landfills, conservation of natural resources, reduced soil erosion, lower electricity and water bills, and increased property values.
